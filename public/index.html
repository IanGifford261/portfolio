About Me!

I'm Ian, and I am a software developer with a background in the military, specifically engineering, as well as retail service work and carpentry for the last three years outside of the USCG. Over that time I was considering getting into coding and the tech industry, but I didn't want to do college courses over 4 years. While researching the software development industry, I had learned how interesting and lucrative this line of business is, as well as a welcoming and tight knit community. I would like to apply this career choice to a progressive type of work environment that is trying to advance technology for the greater good and the future. I would say being a sailor helped me prepare for the mental strain of code while learning to be okay with failure or not knowing what to do.
